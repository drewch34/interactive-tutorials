IF/ELSE DEMO

Think of If/Else statements like a decision tree. What is your program going to do in a certain situation?

These statements require three parts:
1) if - conditional - The situation that might happen in your program.
2) Result - When this situation happens, the program will do this.
3) else - When the situation doesn't happen, the program will do this.

Let's make sure Bit by Bot is dressed appropriately for the weather! Select from the dropdowns below or edit the code!

Remember:
=== means AND (both conditions have to be met to get this result)
|| means OR (one or another condition has to be met to get this result)
